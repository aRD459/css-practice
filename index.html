Diesal is a white and brown Labrador-Mutt thing, and he is the best. He is 3 years old and was born on January 3rd. He is known for the amount of water he loves to spill all over the floor after drinking and thinking he is the size of a lap dog, but actually weighs almost 50 pounds.

Overview of Diesal

Diesal is a very popular boy. He has many friends.
Clearly he is incredibly cute, so it is only natural that he is such a well known guy around the neighborhood.

Diesal's Favorite Foods

  1. Peanut Butter
  2. Watermelon
  3. Eggs

As a note to all dog owners: If your dog also loves peanut butter as much as mine, it is important to be aware that some peanut butter contains an ingredient called "Xylitol" and is actually dangerous for dogs to ingest. Make sure to read the ingredients list on your peanut butter before you give some to your furry friend!
